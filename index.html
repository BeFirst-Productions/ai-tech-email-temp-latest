The Maritime Standard announces the official launch of
TMS AITECH 2026
A dedicated platform focused on artificial intelligence, digital transformation, and the future of intelligent shipping.
Themed “Maritime 4.0 and Beyond: Accelerating Intelligence in Motion” the event comes at a decisive moment as data-driven operations, autonomous systems, predictive analytics, and new IMO digital frameworks begin to redefine how global maritime trade operates.
TMS AITECH 2026 will serve as a central forum for global leaders, innovators, port authorities, shipowners, technology developers, and regulators to discuss the next phase of maritime digitalization.
What AITECH Delivers
• Strategic Clarity
  Understand how AI and digital technologies are reshaping maritime operations, regulation, and competitive advantage.
• Practical Insight
  Gain real-world perspectives on how AI is being applied today across fleets, ports, logistics, and supply chains.
• Regulatory Readiness
  Explore the implications of IMO digital regulations and how to prepare for compliance and future mandates.
 
• Executive Networking
  Connect with senior decision-makers, technology leaders, and industry peers during a focused evening networking cocktail.
• Actionable Direction
  Leave with clear takeaways to support smarter investment, adoption, and governance of AI-driven solutions.
